About Me

Greetings! My name is Aarick Farist. I am an aspiring full-stack web developer; thank you for taking interest in my work! This site is meant to showcase my work and provide a means for you to get in contact with me.

I play Dungeons & Dragons as a Half-Elf Bard, sing and play guitar for a punk band, and enjoy getting lost in an interesting video-game for hours. It is my hope that the creativity I surround myself with will drive me to always make my craft interesting and unique.

To me, web design sits at the perfect intersection of artistic expression and lucrative business opportunities. I intend to make a mark in this industry!
